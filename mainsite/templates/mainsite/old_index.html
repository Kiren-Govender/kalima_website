Cutting edge IT solutions for your Enterprise

 

Looking after an organisations ICT infrastructure is a complex and demanding undertaking. Kalima Technology exists to be of service to all organisations by building a brand agnostic, certified and customer centric business to deliver tangible results.

We focus on three main service deliverables for you.

1. To achieve an attractive return on investment for you.
2. To provide guaranteed, exceptional service.
3. To be the fastest to respond to your requests.

Your requirements are unique, and we understand that. That is why we build solutions tailored to your organisational requirements, choosing from a battery of tried and tested solutions from our alliance partners to achieve a complete, customised and robust solution. We have partnership agreements with IBM, Microsoft, AWS, Cisco, Dell, Lenovo, Fortinet, Kaspersky, Sophos, Trend Micro & Veeam amongst many others to offer you certified expertise & solutions from the worlds leading ICT brands.

Email channel@kalimatech.co.za with your requirements and an expert consultant will be of service to you.
