Blazing performance.

10 x faster than a traditional hard disk drive makes the SSD drive a worthy upgrade to boost system performance of a new system, or to extend the life of an older system. Work with no lag, work with more responsiveness, and you will insist on moving your SSD over to any new system you purchase.

Quiet and cool.

You will be surprised at how noisy the traditional mechanical hard disk drives are, and with the SSD having zero moving parts, noise and friction are at a minimum. This also reduces power consumption, meaning your laptop battery lasts longer.

Reasonable price.

Although the price per gigabyte of an SSD is higher than a traditional hard drive, the SSD is not meant to store your large movie, music and image libraries. Traditional hard drives are more suited for that purpose. SSD drives are for performance, not capacity, and prove their worth when your operating system, software and frequently used files are located on it.

An upgrade to a SSD drive is a lot cheaper than the cost of upgrading your entire system. It is, rand for rand, the cheapest and most cost effective performance upgrade available today.
